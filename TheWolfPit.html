The Wolf Pit

Is a Witcher themed tavern underground in Krakow's Old City.
Going inside feels just like visiting one of the taverns as a Witcher looking for their next job.
Come in, eat the most delicious of meals, craft your own elixir. The tavern has their own currency, dressed up staff that play their role perfectly, and a true adventurer shop!
