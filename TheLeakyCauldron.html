The Leaky Cauldron

Is a Harry Potter themed Cafe underground in Krakow's Old city.
It is a cozy magical place with a whole menu straight from the magical Harry Potter universe! Come in, enjoy some root beer, drink a smoking potion, and at the very end you can even buy a souvenir in the cutest wizard shop!
